# Pretrained Models and Checkpoints

This public demo repository does **not** include large trained model files.

For the full research implementation, approved model checkpoints may be placed in this folder only when licensing, privacy, and ethical requirements permit.

## Expected Model Components

The APO implementation is designed for:

- ViT-B/16 or equivalent vision encoder.
- GPT-4o or equivalent Vision-Language Model API.
- EasyOCR or equivalent OCR module.

## Why Checkpoints Are Not Included

Large trained checkpoints are excluded because:

- they may be too large for normal GitHub hosting;
- pretrained model licenses may restrict redistribution;
- fine-tuned checkpoints may encode patterns from restricted handwriting datasets;
- disability-specific handwriting data may require privacy-preserving handling.
